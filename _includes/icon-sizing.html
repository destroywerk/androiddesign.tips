Icon sizing

Each icon within the Android ecosystem has a specific size in density-independent pixels (dp). Of that area a smaller subset should be the focal area. Iconography design guidelines.

48dp

Launcher icon

  • 48dp assets
  • 48dp focal area
24dp

Action bar icons

  • 32dp assets
  • 24dp focal area
  • #FFFFFF 80%
22dp

Notification icons

  • 24dp assets
  • 22dp focal area
  • #FFFFFF
12

Contextual icons

  • 16dp assets
  • 12dp focal area
